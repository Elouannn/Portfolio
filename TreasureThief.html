The Explorer's Chest

Title : The Explorer's Chest

Type :VR Coop assymetrical riddle game

Plateforme : VR Duo only (1 VR set)

Public : All public, casual friendly

Made with : Unity

Play time : around 15 minutes

Origin : School project

Controls : One player with VR set, the other one with a printed document


Contact me to download it (free)

Important

This game is made for two players, it's base on the system of "Keep Talking And Nobody Explode". It means that one player as the VR headset, and the other one guides him through the riddles.

The game

You heard about a mystical treasure hidden in this room... Be quick to find it before the owner comes back !
