How Not To Be A God

Title : How Not To Be A God

Type : City builder

Plateforme : PC Solo

Public : 12+ (violence), casual friendly

Made with : Unreal Engine 5

Play time : <15/20 minutes

Origin : Graduation Project (solo dev)

The game

Try to make this civilisation grow without interacting direclty with your dumb as f*** peoples, i'm sure you won't dream to be god anymore after that !
