Foam's Up!'

Title : Foam's Up!'

Plateforme : PC ( Versus or Solo ) and Mobile (Solo only )

Public : All ages, casual friendly

Made with : Godot

Play time : <5 minutes / partie

Origin : Global Game Jam - Dijon - 48h

Controls :Player 1: WASD / ZQSD, Mouse, finger (Digital Devices) or Gamepad 1
Player 2: Arrows , or Gamepad 2 (or controlled by Bot)


Play it in the browser (down bellow)

Important

If you do not have a friend to play with, you can select a bot in the character selection menu. Try to defeat all 3 levels of bots !

The game

Here is our game made during the Global Game Jam 2025, on the theme : Bubbles.
Take on your drinking partner or the King of the Tavern, choose your favorite beer, and foam your way to victory !🍻
