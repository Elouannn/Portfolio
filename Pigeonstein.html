Pigeonstein

Title : Pigeonstein

Type : Party game

Plateforme : PC Coop

Public : 16+ (violence), casual friendly

Made with : Godot

Play time : <5 minutes / game

Origin : Global Game Jam 2024 - Dijon - 48h

Controls : Left Wing: ZQSD/WQSD to move and SPACE/A/E to hit
Right Wing: ARROWS/IJKL to move and ENTER/U/O to hit


Play it in browser at this link : Itch.io

Important

This game is GRAPHIC about DEATH, BLOOD and VIOLENCE involving cartoony characters.

The game

Pigeonstein is a 2 players co-op/versus game about a pigeon and a dove, both in love with each other. But when they were crossing the road to finally meet properly, they were both hit by a car and cut in half. But their love was stronger than this and they fused to create the ultimate bird:
THE PIGEONSTEIN!
With their newly acquired power, they decide to take revenge on the monsters that dare to step on them! The pigeon use its sharp bone to pierce through monsters feet, and the dove use its soft feather to tickle them.
Each player control one side of the same body, forcing players to team up in order to survive. Be the best side and score the most point!
